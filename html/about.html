About Us

Our story


Published in 2021, PetLook PH was created by a group of animal lovers who determined to help and address the unfortunate situations of different household pets in the Philippines.


One of our goals is to help the rescued and voluntary surrendered pets to have their new home and responsible owners. PetLook PH makes it easier for the potential adopters to look for their new pet from available animal shelters. You can find and message us at https://www.petlookph.com/ or Email: petlookph@gmail.com.

Disclaimer: PetLook PH does not run an animal shelter or organization. We are only a directory of homeless and rescued animals from different pet adoption organizations. Although the information on our website is updated frequently, it is always best to contact the shelter or the organization that lists the pet to check its additional information.(demo)

MISSION

To promote animal welfare in the country through education, public awareness, and affordable sterilization activities

VISION

To make the Philippines, a country where all cats, dogs, and other household pets have good homes and responsible owners
